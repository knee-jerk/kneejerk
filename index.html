How does
KneeJerk
work?

Six prediction categories. Pick early and you score more points if you're right. Wait and see how things play out, but every day costs you.

You can change your mind as many times as you like — but each change locks in that day's lower value. The game rewards confidence and conviction.

1

Make your picks

Send your predictions to the league admin before the tournament starts. The earlier you pick, the more points a correct answer is worth.

2

Change your mind

You can update any pick right up to the day before it resolves. Just tell the admin. But the value drops every day — so changing late costs you.

3

Sunday updates

The dashboard refreshes every Sunday at 09:00 BST. Changes made during the week don't show until Sunday — so nobody can copy your last-minute thinking.

4

Score when categories resolve

Group stage categories resolve 27 June. Everything else resolves after the final, 19 July. Ties pay full value to everyone who picked correctly.
