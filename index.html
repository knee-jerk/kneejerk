How does
KneeJerk
work?

Make six predictions before the World Cup. Once the tournament begins, change your mind as many times as you want — right up to the day before the final, even!

However... there's a cost. Each day that passes, the value of a prediction goes down. A prediction made at the start of the tournament scores more points than one at the end.

But you only score points if the prediction is right.

And this is the conundrum that Knee-jerkers face. Stay loyal to a high-value pick you've lost hope with? Or make a change that feels right but for points that feel wrong?

Decision-making is everything in football.

Make your picks

Change your mind

You can update your pick right up to the day that category resolves. Send any new pick to the admin — the potential points value will also update.

3

Weekly updates

The dashboard refreshes every Sunday at 9:00 BST, at which point changes made during the week will be made public on the dashboard and individual category pages.

4

Points scoring

Group stage categories resolve on 27th June. All other categories resolve on 19th July after the World Cup final. In the event of category ties, points can be awarded for multiple correct picks.
