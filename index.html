How it Works

A quick guide to KneeJerk

The idea

KneeJerk is a private prediction game for the FIFA World Cup 2026. You compete with the rest of your league across six prediction categories. The more confident and the earlier you commit, the more points you can score, but the more brutal it is when you get it wrong.

The six categories

  • Most Group Stage Points — pick the team with the highest group stage points total
  • Least Group Stage Points — pick the team with the lowest group stage points total
  • Top Scoring Team — pick the team that scores the most goals across the tournament
  • Most Assists — pick the player with the most assists
  • Golden Boot — pick the tournament's top goalscorer
  • World Cup Winners — pick the team that lifts the trophy

The scoring

Before the tournament begins, every category has its maximum points value. Every day from kick-off (11 June 2026), the value drops. The points you score for a correct prediction are the points that were available the day you locked that pick in. Change your mind later and you're locking in fewer points, even if you turn out to be right.

Changing your predictions

You can change any prediction as many times as you like, right up to the day before that category resolves. Each change overwrites the previous pick and locks in that day's lower points value. The Group Stage categories lock when the group stage finishes (26 June). All other categories lock the day before the final (18 July).

Submitting a change

Predictions are submitted to the league admin outside of this site. To make or change a pick, message the admin directly. The dashboard refreshes every Sunday at 09:00 BST, so changes you make during the week appear on Sunday morning. This stops anyone copying late-week reactions.

Ties and edge cases

  • If multiple teams or players tie for the top spot in a category (for example, three teams sharing the same group stage points total), anyone who picked any of the tied options scores their full locked-in value. No splits.
  • If you don't pick anything for a category, you score 0 for that category. The dashboard will show "No pick".
  • World Cup Winners cannot tie. There's only one trophy.

How the league is won

At the end of the tournament, when all six categories have resolved, the player with the highest total of actual scored points wins the league. While the tournament is running, the dashboard shows your potential points (the sum of your locked-in values, before resolution) alongside the scored points for any categories that have already been decided.
