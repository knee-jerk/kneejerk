Week 3 Verdicts
PM
Paul "The Gaffer" Meyers
Effortlessly correct
A man so ridiculously good looking, so effortlessly successful and so unnervingly competent at absolutely everything he attempts, that scientists are genuinely stumped. Legend has it he predicted the 2014 World Cup winner while simultaneously closing a seven-figure deal, restoring a classic car and fixing next door's lawnmower. Paul's predictions are considered so accurate that bookmakers have banned him from three continents. He is here to grudgingly share his wisdom with mere mortals.
DH
Dave "YNWA" Hodgson
Slot sceptic in chief
Liverpool obsessive, Slot sceptic, and the only man on Merseyside who can turn a 3-0 home win into a 40-minute rant about tactical rigidity. Dave's predictions are 60% football analysis and 40% veiled complaint about whichever Liverpool manager currently isn't Jürgen Klopp. If England are playing, expect tactical breakdowns. If Liverpool are playing, expect a lecture on how it would all be better under someone, anyone, else.
DB
Dave "Two Flags" Broome
Nationality varies
Staunch Boro fan, proud Teessider, and a man whose nationality depends entirely on how the Three Lions or the Tartan Army are getting on. Loses on penalties to Germany? Always been Scottish, nan was from Oban. Scotland crash out in the group stage? English through and through, pass the flag. His World Cup predictions are famously hedged, famously contradictory, and famously wrong, but always delivered with total conviction in whichever accent feels appropriate on the day.
